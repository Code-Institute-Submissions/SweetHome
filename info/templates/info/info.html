How it works

Who doesn't want to have there own Home Sweet Home?
Everybody offcourse, but where to look?
We've got the answer for you, Sweet Home!

On this website you can find sweet Homes in Boxtel, Den Bosch, Drunen, Heeswijk-Dinter, Liempde, Oss and Rosmalen.
There is a perfect home for everybody!
But how does it work?

If you click on "Houses" in the menu, you will land on the Houses page.
On this page you can see all the available houses and if wanted you can set a filter for easy searching.
You can see a photo of the front of the house, the address and the price, but also the closing date.
The closing date tells you until what day it is possible to subscribe to the house when interested.
After this closing date there will be a lottery to decide who will get the first change to come and view the house.
That person will then have the change to take the house, otherwise the next in line will get the opportunity, etc.

Ok, so what if you want to subscribe to a house that you like?
First you will have to log in, or subscribe to the page. Then we will ask you to provide us with your personal details on the profile page.
When that's done, a button will be available to buy a one year subscription for €25.
After this payment you can click on a house, where you will find more photo's, information and you will see a subscription button has become available.
When you've used the subscribe button you can always unsubscribe in the same place.
Also, after subscribing you will land on your profile page, where you can now find your subscription.
The subscriptions will be viewed here until the closing date has gone by.
In this place you can also unsubscribe, or link back to the houses info page.

In case something is still unclear you can allways use the contact page to get in touch with us.
But, if everything ís clear, then we wish you the best of luck in finding your own Home Sweet Home!
